# Educational Amplifier Model
## What is it ? üìî
This is a model used to teach students about the working principle of a integrated linear amplifier.
## Features ‚úÖ
-  3 1KOhm resistors
-  2 2KOhm resistors
-  1 3KOhm resistor
-  2 5KOhm resistors
-  2 10KOhm resistors.
-  2 100 nF non-polarized capacitors
-  10K Potentiometer
-  TL081 Amplifier IC
## Why did I make it ? üõ†Ô∏è
My electrical engineering teacher had some models provided by the ministry of education. But they had tiny 2mm banana terminals, and were very ugly-looking with a low quality PCB on top, mounted on a cheap electrical box.

When I showed him a project I made before, using laser-cut and engraved MDF wood, he asked me to partener up with him and make a new, better-looking, more functional model, with 4mm banana temrinals.
